Deyi Ji

I am a senior researcher at Alibaba DAMO Academy and Alibaba Cloud Computing Ltd., where I works with Prof. Jieping Ye and Xian-Sheng Hua on deep learning and computer vision. I now also study for a Doctor's degree at University of Science and Technology of China, by the “Doctoral Program for Future Technology Leading Talents in National Key Fields”, co-advised by Prof. Feng Zhao and Prof. Feng Wu. Before that, I worked as a researcher at SenseTime with Wei Wu and Junjie Yan, in Prof. Xiaogang Wang's team. I received my Master's degree of Computer Science in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I was advised by Prof. Hongtao Lu, and Bachelor's degree in Huazhong University of Science and Technology co-advised by Prof. Yu Liu and Xinggang Wang.
